they make sense for that device — some are everywhere, a few are specific to
where they're most useful.

## What the columns mean

* **Web** — [web.stremio.com](https://web.stremio.com) in any browser.
* **Desktop** — the Stremio **v5** apps for Windows and macOS (Linux support coming soon).
* **Mobile** — Android phones and tablets, iOS and iPadOS.
* **TV** — Android TV, Samsung (Tizen) and LG (webOS) smart TVs.
* **VR** — Meta Quest and Pico headsets.

> **Note:** Apple apps (iOS, iPadOS, macOS, tvOS, visionOS) follow the same availability as
> their platform class — an iPhone behaves like Mobile, an Apple TV like TV.

## Why some features are "view only" on TV

A TV remote isn't great for drag-and-drop, renaming and PIN entry. So for
[Organize Content](../features/organize-content.md),
[Filter Streams](../features/filter-streams.md) and
[Profiles Management](../features/profiles-management.md), TVs **apply and use**
your setup but you create and edit it once on a phone or computer. Because every
setting syncs to your Stremio account, it shows up on the TV automatically.
