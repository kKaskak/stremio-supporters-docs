# Stremio Supporters

![](.gitbook/assets/logo.png)

**Supporters** is Stremio's paid tier. It keeps the apps free for everyone and
unlocks a set of features that make watching calmer, faster and more personal —
across every device you use Stremio on.

This handbook explains every Supporters feature, what it does, and how to use it
on each platform.

> **Note:** Everything in Stremio stays free. Supporters features are additions on top of
> the standard experience — nothing that was free becomes paid.

## Becoming a Supporter

1. Go to [stremio.com](https://www.stremio.com) and **log in** to the Stremio
   account you want to make a Supporter.
2. Open the [stremio.com/donate](https://www.stremio.com/donate) page.
3. **Select the plan** you want to subscribe to.

Supporter status is tied to your Stremio account, so every device you sign into
unlocks the features above automatically.

Once active, Supporters features appear throughout the apps — in the player, in
Settings, and on the discovery pages — wherever they're relevant.
