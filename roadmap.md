# Roadmap

![](.gitbook/assets/hero-roadmap.png)

> What's next for Supporters.

These features are in active development. Details may change as they ship, but
here's where Supporters is heading.

### 📁 Library Management & Folders

Organize your library into folders — group shows, films and collections the way
you think about them, instead of one long list.

### 💬 More Subtitle Improvements

Many more subtitle upgrades on the way, building on
[live sync](features/improved-subtitles.md).

### 🎨 Themes for all apps

Personalize the look of every Stremio app, across all platforms.

### 🎬 Watch Together & VR Cinema

Watch in sync with friends — including a premium VR cinema environment on Meta
Quest and Pico — coming across more of Stremio's apps.

---

Have an idea? Supporters help shape what comes next. Share requests through the
in-app feedback options or at [stremio.com](https://www.stremio.com).
