# FAQ

> Common questions about Supporters billing, accounts and features.

### If I cancel, forget to pay or otherwise lose access, are my settings erased?

No. All your content, settings, user profiles and everything else are kept
safe. They stay in place and come back as soon as you resubscribe.

### I paid on the wrong Stremio account / email.

Contact our team at [support@stremio.com](mailto:support@stremio.com) and we'll
move the subscription over to the correct Stremio account seamlessly. We can
also change the email used for your transaction confirmations.

### How do I cancel?

Go to [stremio.com](https://www.stremio.com) → **Manage billing** and cancel
there.

### Can I import my watch history, library and other items from another Stremio account into a new profile?

> **Coming soon.** From [stremio.com/acc-settings](https://www.stremio.com/acc-settings)
> you'll be able to migrate everything from an external Stremio account into a
> profile. You can pick an existing profile, or choose **migrate into a new
> profile** and one will be created for you automatically.

### I can only see Cancel in Manage billing — there's no button on the donate page to upgrade or change my subscription.

> **Coming soon.** You'll be able to switch seamlessly between monthly, yearly
> and one-time lifetime billing.

### Some features don't work.

Submit a bug report on
[stremio-bugs](https://github.com/Stremio/stremio-bugs) and we'll take a look.
